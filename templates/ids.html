What Is Being Analyzed?
Network Packet Flows
Bidirectional traffic flows between source and destination, captured and processed by CICFlowMeter.
Connection Duration
Lifetime of each TCP/UDP session in microseconds — key indicator of attacks like Slowloris.
Bytes Sent / Received
Total and per-packet byte counts in both forward and backward directions of the flow.
Packet Timing Patterns
Inter-Arrival Time (IAT) statistics — mean, std, max, min — reveal rhythm of communication.
Protocol Behavior
TCP flag counts (SYN, ACK, FIN, RST, PSH) expose handshake anomalies and scan patterns.
Flow Rate Statistics
Bytes/s and Packets/s ratios identify flooding attacks through abnormally high throughput values.
